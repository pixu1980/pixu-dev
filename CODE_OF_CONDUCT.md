# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be useful, respectful, and welcoming.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind and direct.
- Assume good intent.
- Keep feedback about the work.
- Respect accessibility, privacy, and consent.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information.
- Repeated disruption after being asked to stop.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.
